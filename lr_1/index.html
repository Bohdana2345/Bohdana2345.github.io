about

We Love What We Do and Do What We Love!

In 1992 our store was founded in Ivano-Frankinsk. Our aim is to become the top eastern sweets brand in the country. We began by importing authentic eastern sweets and now produce it in our factory. We supply our products across the country, both wholesale and retail, to private customers and businesses. We emphasize high-quality products at fair prices. We understand the importance of receiving your order swiftly and guarantee prompt delivery to your doorstep. Our quality standards are exceptionally high, ensuring that you always receive the best products on the market. At Sultan, we're not just selling sweets; we're offering a piece of culture, a taste of tradition, and an unmatched shopping experience. This is all bound together by our love for eastern sweets and our commitment to our customers.
